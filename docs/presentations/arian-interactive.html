Why Traditional Scoring Fails

The Spray-and-Pray Problem

We have 2,800 accounts using partner technologies. Traditional lead scoring assigns a single score to each based on traffic, size, and tech signals.

But a SaaS company with high traffic scores the same as a Fashion retailer with high traffic. The data shows Fashion delivers 65% of all proof points. SaaS delivers zero.

  • All accounts treated equally
  • Wasted AE time on low-fit accounts
  • Unclear prioritization
  • Low response rates
  • Missed high-value opportunities
